Teacher Guide

A simple plan for an active two-hour English class.

1
Start with the 24-mission adventure.
It rotates vocabulary, memory, listening, movement, spelling and grammar.
2
Use the speaking pause.
After a correct answer, ask Antonio to repeat, make a short sentence or show the action.
3
Keep captions off.
The answer pictures do not show the target word, so Antonio must truly recognise it.
4
Take a movement break with Move & Say.
Regular and irregular verbs include visual clues and past forms.
5
Finish with Mega Boss.
It mixes vocabulary, listening, grammar, spelling and verb forms.
